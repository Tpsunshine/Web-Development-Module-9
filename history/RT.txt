 Rabindranath Tagore was a legendary Indian poet. Furthermore, he was also a great
        philosopher, patriot, painter, and humanist. People often made use of the word Gurudev with regard to
        him. This exceptional personality was born on the 7th of May in 1861 at Calcutta. His early education
        took place at home by a variety of teachers. Also, through this education, he got knowledge of many
        subjects. His higher education took place in England. Above all, Rabindranath Tagore began writing poems
        from a very young age.
